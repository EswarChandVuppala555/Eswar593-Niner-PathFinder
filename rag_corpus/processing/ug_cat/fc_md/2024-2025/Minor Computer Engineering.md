| * * * |

The Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering offers a Minor in Computer Engineering for non-majors.

All program-level Admissions and Progression Requirements are in addition to the [University of North Carolina at Charlotte Admission Requirements](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/content.php?catoid=38&navoid=4461). |
| ## Admission Requirements<br>* * *<br>#### Current UNC Charlotte Undergraduate Students<br>- See [University Admission Requirements](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/content.php?catoid=38&navoid=4461)<br>## Prerequisite Courses<br>* * *<br>The minor assumes that students have a background in mathematics that is covered in the first year of the ECGR curriculum. In mathematics, this would cover Calculus ( [MATH 1120](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#tt4654), [MATH 1121](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#tt1009), or [MATH 1241](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#tt648)). The totality of such courses is 3 or more credit hours.<br>## Minor Requirements<br>* * *<br>The Minor in Computer Engineering consists of 15 additional credit hours and requires the following:<br>### Required Courses (12 credit hours)<br>* * *<br>- [ECGR 2104 - Computer Engineering Programming II](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 2181 - Logic Systems Design](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 3101 - Embedded Systems](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 3183 - Computer Organization](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>### Elective Course (3 credit hours)<br>* * *<br>_Select at least one of the following:_<br>- [ECGR 4100 - Research Tools and Techniques in Computer Engineering](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 4101 - Advanced Embedded Systems](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 4105 - Introduction to Machine Learning](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 4106 - Real-Time Machine Learning](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 4146 - Introduction to VHDL](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 4161 - Introduction to Robotics](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>- [ECGR 4181 - Computer Architecture](https://catalog.charlotte.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=38&poid=10713#) (3)<br>## Total = 15 Credit Hours<br>* * *<br>## Progression Requirements<br>* * *<br>Students must achieve a minimum GPA of 2.00 in all Minor courses.<br>## Special Policies or Requirements<br>* * *<br>This minor is not available for a student who has a major in either Electrical Engineering or Computer Engineering. |
